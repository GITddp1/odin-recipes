Birria

A Mexican Take On Beef Stew With Blended Chile Peppers!

Ingredients

  • 5 dried Anaheim chile peppers, stemmed and seeded
  • 5 guajillo chile peppers, stemmed and seeded
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1 tbsp mixed spices, or to taste
  • 1 tbsp salt, or to taste
  • 3 lbs cubed beef stew meat
  • 6 bay leaves
  • water filled to cover all

Steps

  1. fill saucepan with anaheim and guajillo peppers
  2. cover saucepan contents with water
  3. bring water and contents to a boil
  4. once boiling, reduce heat to simmer until tender (roughly 15 mins)
  5. once tender, set aside to cool for 5 mins
  6. transfer chiles and water into blender with onion, mixed spices, and salt
  7. blend contents until smooth
  8. add stew meat into large pot
  9. stir in pureed chile mix with bay leaves
  10. cook over medium-low until meat is very tender (roughly 3-5 hrs)

Serve Your Freshly Finished Dish - Enjoy!
