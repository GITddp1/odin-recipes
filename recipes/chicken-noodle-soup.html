Chicken Noodle Soup

Quick & Easy Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup

This recipe will contain natural ingredients that are easy to find and that you may already have!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 14.5 oz chicken broth (canned)
  • 14.5 oz vegetable broth (canned)
  • 1/2 lb chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 1/2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • salt and ground pepper to taste

Steps

  1. melt butter in a large pot over medium heat
  2. add onion and celery, cook until just tender (roughly 5 mins)
  3. add chicken broth, vegetable brother, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper
  4. stir to combine while bringing to a boil
  5. reduce heat and simmer for 20 mins

Serve Your Freshly Finished Dish - Enjoy!
